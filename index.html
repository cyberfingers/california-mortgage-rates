Mortgage Options Specific to California

  • California 30-Year Fixed: The most popular loan for buying and refinancing, offering lower monthly payments and the option to buy with as little as 3% down.
  • California 15-Year Fixed: Provides faster payoff and less interest paid over the life of the loan, with as little as 5% down for home purchases.
  • California FHA Loan: Flexible loans with lower down payments ideal for first-time California homebuyers.
  • California Jumbo Loans: Financing solutions for higher-value homes prevalent in California.
  • California VA Loans: Zero-down mortgage solutions for eligible veterans and service members in California.
  • California Adjustable-Rate Mortgage (ARM): Starts with a fixed interest rate for 5, 7, or 10 years, then adjusts every 6 months, often at a lower initial rate.
  • California Home Equity Loan: Borrow a lump sum from your home's equity at a fixed interest rate with predictable monthly payments, without refinancing.
  • California ONE+ by Rocket Mortgage: Designed to make home buying more affordable, this program allows you to purchase with just 1% down, and Rocket Mortgage covers an additional 2%.
  • California HomeReady® and Home Possible®: Affordable loan options with just 3% down and reduced mortgage insurance for qualified buyers.

Special Assistance Programs in California

  • California Public Servant Mortgage Programs: Mortgage programs designed specifically for California's educators, first responders, healthcare workers, and public servants, offering favorable loan conditions.
  • California Down Payment Assistance: Rocket Mortgage accepts many down payment assistance programs to help make homeownership more accessible for qualified buyers.
  • California RentRewards: Eligible renters may save up to $5,000 in closing costs when buying a home with Rocket Mortgage.
  • California Native American Home Loans: Multiple loan options are available for fee simple-owned property on Native American reservations.
  • Property Tax Relief OptPions for LA Fire Victims: The Assessor's Information Packet for disaster relief for California residents.

Mortgage Requirements and Services in California

  • Escrow Waivers (Impound Waivers): Allows qualified California homeowners to directly control property tax and insurance payments, providing greater financial flexibility.
  • Transparent Refinancing Costs (SB2 Fees): Clearly communicated refinancing recording fees adhering to California's Senate Bill 2 (SB2), ensuring transparency and compliance.
  • California Finance Lender Disclosure: Mandatory disclosure highlighting the role of mortgage brokers to ensure transparent and compliant lending practices according to California law.
  • California Property Tax Estimates: Provides accurate estimations of property taxes (approximately 1.25% of the home's purchase price) including reassessment considerations, helping California homeowners plan financially.
  • California Fair Plan Insurance: Named-peril property insurance essential for homes located in California's wildfire-prone regions, seamlessly integrated with your mortgage.
